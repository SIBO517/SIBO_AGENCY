About SIBO Agency

SIBO AGENCY is a registered sales and marketing company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialize in bridging the gap between businesses needing skilled sales personnel and individuals seeking sales opportunities.

Our mission is to empower businesses with exceptional sales talent and create fulfilling sales careers, fostering growth and prosperity for all stakeholders.

We envision becoming the leading provider of outsourced sales solutions in Kenya, recognized for our professionalism, results-driven approach, and commitment to excellence.

Our Services

We provide highly vetted and trained sales professionals to clients on a contractual or project basis across various industries.

Shop Attendants

Providing trained personnel to manage retail spaces, assist customers, and drive in-store sales.

Direct Sales Agents

Deploying agents for door-to-door sales, field sales, and direct consumer engagement initiatives.

Promoters/Brand Ambassadors

Supplying individuals to represent brands, conduct demos, and engage with customers at events.

Merchandisers

Offering personnel to optimize product placement, manage inventory, and ensure visual appeal.

Telesales Agents

Providing trained individuals for outbound/inbound calls, lead generation, and customer service.

Event Sales Staff

B2B Sales Representatives

Specialized sales personnel to target other businesses and drive corporate sales.

Sales Team Management

Sales Training

Providing bespoke training programs for sales teams and client companies.

Sales Analytics

Delivering reports on sales performance, market insights, and optimization recommendations.
